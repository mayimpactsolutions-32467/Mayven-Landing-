What We Do

We don't start with what you do.
We start with who you are.

The Positioning Foundation is a strategic engagement designed to extract the core narrative of a founder or CEO and build the messaging infrastructure that everything else in their public life stands on.

This is not a branding exercise. It is not a content strategy session. This is the deep, structured work of identifying the philosophy embedded in your decisions, your leadership, and your life, and translating that into language you can use everywhere.

The goal is simple: when you read the final document, you should feel like someone described you better than you could describe yourself.

A branding agency will interview you for an hour and deliver a positioning statement that could belong to any founder in your industry.

We begin with a deep discovery process where the goal is not to document what you say, but to identify the patterns in how you think, how you lead, and how you make decisions. The things you believe so deeply that you've never thought to articulate them. That's what we extract. That's what we build on. And that's why the final document sounds like you, not like a brand.

The Process

Four phases.
Three to four weeks.

1
Discovery Session (90 minutes)
A deep, recorded conversation where I listen for what's underneath the surface. This is not a standard interview. You talk. I listen for the philosophy, the patterns, and the breakthrough concepts that define who you are as a leader.
2
Research & Intelligence Building
Using the discovery transcript, I build a comprehensive intelligence profile: your core identity, your language patterns, the moments where your voice changes because you're saying something you genuinely believe, and the gaps where your thinking is still unresolved.
3
Deliverable Production
I build your Positioning Foundation document. Every element is built from your specific identity, your language, and your philosophy. The document goes through multiple rounds of internal refinement before you ever see it.
4
Refinement + 30-Day Support
After you review the initial document, we meet for a focused 60-minute refinement session. Your reactions and instincts shape the final version. Then you have 30 days of direct access for adjustments and implementation guidance.
